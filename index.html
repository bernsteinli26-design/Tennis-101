Welcome to Tennis Training

Tennis is a global sport that combines athletic skill, strategy, and mental focus. It can be played recreationally or competitively and is enjoyed by millions of people worldwide.

Quick Facts About Tennis

  • Tennis was originally played using hands before rackets were invented
  • The scoring term "love" means zero
  • Serves can reach speeds over 120 mph
  • Tennis is played on hard, clay, and grass courts
