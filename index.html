Tennis Training Hub

Tennis is a global sport played by millions of people of all ages. It combines physical endurance, strategy, and mental focus. From beginners learning how to serve to professionals competing at Grand Slams, tennis offers something for everyone.

About Tennis

Tennis can be played in singles or doubles. The objective is to hit the ball over the net and into the opponent’s court in a way they cannot return it. Matches are played in sets, and players must win a certain number of games to win a set.

Benefits of Playing Tennis

  • Improves cardiovascular fitness
  • Builds coordination and agility
  • Develops focus and discipline
  • Encourages lifelong physical activity
