Tennis Techniques

Forehand

The forehand is one of the most powerful shots. Players should focus on foot positioning and follow-through for accuracy.

Backhand

The backhand can be hit with one or two hands. A strong backhand helps maintain control during rallies.

Serve

The serve starts every point and can be a major advantage. Players use flat, slice, or topspin serves.

Volley

Volleys are shots hit near the net without letting the ball bounce. They require quick reflexes.
