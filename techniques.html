Forehand

The forehand is your main shot. Use your whole body to generate power and follow through.

Backhand

The backhand can be one-handed or two-handed and helps maintain control during rallies.

Serve

The serve starts every point. Practice accuracy and consistency before power.

Volley

Volleys are hit before the ball bounces and require quick reactions at the net.
