Tennis Basics

Start your journey the right way

Getting Started

Tennis is one of the most popular sports in the world. Beginners focus on learning how to control the ball, move around the court, and understand the rules.

Court Layout

The tennis court includes baselines, service boxes, and sidelines. Players must hit the ball within these lines to keep the rally going.

Basic Rules

  • Hit the ball over the net
  • Ball can bounce once
  • Keep the ball inside the lines
  • Win points when opponent misses

Scoring

Scoring goes: Love, 15, 30, 40, Game. At 40-40, it becomes deuce and a player must win by 2 points.

Beginner Tips

  • Focus on control, not power
  • Practice footwork daily
  • Watch the ball carefully
  • Stay relaxed while playing
