Tennis Basics

Court & Rules

A tennis court is divided into two sides by a net. Players must hit the ball within the boundary lines. Matches can be played on different surfaces like grass, clay, or hard courts.

Scoring System

Tennis scoring is unique: 0 (love), 15, 30, 40, and game. If both players reach 40, it becomes "deuce," and a player must win two consecutive points.

Types of Matches

Singles involves one player on each side, while doubles includes teams of two. Doubles courts are wider and require teamwork and communication.
