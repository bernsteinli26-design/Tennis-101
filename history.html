History of Tennis

Tennis began in 12th century France as "jeu de paume," played with hands. Rackets were later introduced.

Modern tennis developed in England in the 1800s. The first Wimbledon tournament was held in 1877.

Today, tennis is played worldwide and is part of the Olympic Games, with major tournaments called Grand Slams.

Growth of the Sport

Tennis spread globally due to its inclusion in international competitions and professional tours. It is now played at both amateur and professional levels.
